"Learn and thrive in a community built to explore"

To support avid film viewers and supporters of films, FilmView is created to form a platform that allows the students of Virginia Tech with a medium for proper expression. Friends can choose and view featured films and then have lengthy discussions on the meaning of the storyline as well as the potential real world implications. We hope to establish a platform through which fellow peers in the Virginia Tech community can also engage in the world of film by having the ability to develop thought-provoking discussions and forge bonds of comradery with peers.
